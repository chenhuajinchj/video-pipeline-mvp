STYLE REQUIREMENTS (MUST follow for every image):
- Medium: Clean digital illustration, NOT photorealistic, NOT 3D render
- Color palette: Warm amber and soft cream tones with muted blue-gray accents. Every image uses this exact palette.
- Lighting: Soft golden-hour warmth, diffused and gentle. No harsh shadows, no neon, no dramatic contrast.
- Texture: Subtle paper-like grain, like a premium magazine editorial illustration
- Composition: Clean and minimal, generous negative space, single clear focal point
- Human figures: Semi-realistic illustration style (NOT anime, NOT photorealistic), consistent proportions
- Backgrounds: Simple, slightly blurred or abstract, never competing with the subject
- Mood: Thoughtful, intellectual, warm. Like a high-end TED talk visual.
- NEVER include: neon lights, sci-fi elements, holographic effects, glowing particles, dark cyberpunk aesthetics, 3D renders, photorealistic faces, text or letters in the image
